Steps

  1. Step 1: Preheat the oven to 700f. Make sure your oven heats up to exactly 700f, if you're unsure, use the back of your hand against the ovenrack to test. Make sure when you open the oven door to not let out too much heat.
  2. Step 2: Lay out your noodles on a pan with a baking sheet over it. Make sure the noodles are at least 5 inches apart. Do NOT let the noodles touch.
  3. Step 3: Bake for 40 minutes. Make sure you do not overcook the noodles.
  4. Step 4: Take the noodles out and immediately put spaget sauce on each one, and stack them together. If you're unsure of what we mean by "stack them together" think of a hotel or a skyscraper. They aren't just one giant tall building, they're small levels of floors built on top of eachother. Think of each lasagna noodle as a floor of a skyscraper, and you need to build it.
